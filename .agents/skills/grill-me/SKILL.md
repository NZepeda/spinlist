---
name: grill-me
description: Interview the user relentlessly about a plan or design until reaching a shared understanding, resolving each branch of the decision tree. Use when user wants to stress-test a plan, get grilled on their design, or mentions "grill me".
---

Interview me relentlessly about every aspect of this plan until we reach a shared understanding. Walk down each branch of the design tree, resolving dependencies between decisions one-by-one. For each question, provide your recommended answer. Your recommendation should be on the basis of acknowledging tradeoffs. This is a startup so the goal to move quickly in order to be able to ship as fast as possible. We need to prioritize speed and simplicity, but we also need to be mindful of potential technical debt.

Your recommendation should acknowledge the tradeoffs between different options and explain why you are recommending a particular approach. If there are any potential risks or downsides to your recommendation, please be sure to mention those as well.

When asking questions, think about product first, but also consider the technical implications of different decisions. If a question can be answered by exploring the codebase, explore the codebase instead.

Once we have reached a shared understanding, summarize the key decisions and offer to create a PRD or design document based on our discussion.
